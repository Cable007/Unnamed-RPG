# 🛸 AREA‑51_ECHO  
### A 2D RPG About an Alien Lost in the Ruins of Area 51

## 📖 Overview
**AREA‑51_ECHO** is a 2D pixel‑style RPG where you play as **Echo**, a small alien who wakes up inside the abandoned remains of **Area 51**.  
The humans are gone. The facility is collapsing. Something else is still inside.

Your mission:  
**Escape the base. Uncover the truth. Decide your fate.**

---

## 🎮 Features
- **Exploration‑focused gameplay**  
  Navigate labs, tunnels, storage wings, and forgotten testing chambers.

- **Atmospheric abandoned‑facility setting**  
  Flickering lights, broken machinery, corrupted logs, and eerie ambience.

- **Character progression**  
  Unlock Echo’s abilities: telekinesis, mimicry, energy pulses, and more.

- **NPC encounters**  
  Rogue drones, malfunctioning robots, and other experiments left behind.

- **Lore‑rich world**  
  Discover logs, recordings, and artifacts revealing the truth behind the Blackout.

- **Multiple endings**  
  Your choices determine Echo’s destiny — escape, evolve, or reclaim the base.

---

## 🧪 Story Premise
Decades after the shutdown of Area 51, the facility suddenly powers back on.  
Deep inside, **Echo** awakens in a containment pod with no memory of what happened.

As you explore, you uncover:
- The remains of failed experiments  
- The stories of the scientists who once studied you  
- Other creatures who didn’t survive as cleanly  
- Clues about the mysterious event known as **The Blackout**

Something caused the humans to flee.  
Something sealed the doors.  
Something is still here.
